A place where time doesn’t exist

Diary Entry

Today feels like it’s fading in and out. I couldn’t sleep last night, and I ended up sitting by the window staring at the sky. Everything felt quieter than usual.

Maybe it’s just me or maybe it’s the city. But that feeling… the one where everything doesn’t quite make sense? Yeah. I have that.
